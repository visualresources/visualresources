About

Born in Philly. Built for the whole country.

Visual Resources started in 2011 with one client, one storefront, and a lot of phone calls. Fifteen years later, it's still run by the same person — me — and that's on purpose.

When you hire a big sign company, your project passes through a salesperson, a project manager, a designer, and a subcontractor. When you hire me, you talk to the person who designs it, sources it, checks it, and makes sure it goes up straight.

Over the years I've built a trusted network of printers, fabricators, and installers across the country. That means big-shop capability with small-shop accountability — whether it's one storefront in Philly or a fifty-location national rollout.
